Associate in Computer Technology (ACT)

Background

I am currently a student of the Associated Computer Technology (ACT) program. Throughout my studies, I have gained foundational skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and C#, which have equipped me to create functional and visually appealing websites.

Experience with This Final Project

This final project challenged me to design and develop a full, multi-page business profile website for a fictional company called ACTIVION Studios. It gave me hands-on experience integrating responsive design with Tailwind CSS, adding interactive JavaScript features, and focusing on user experience similar to professional industry standards.

Reflection on My Design Process

Working on this project taught me the importance of planning, consistency, and adaptability. I began by researching similar business websites to understand layout and design flow, then created wireframes before coding. Throughout development, I iterated based on feedback, ensuring accessibility and responsiveness. This experience has deepened my appreciation for detail and polished user interfaces, and I am proud of the skills I have honed during this final project.
